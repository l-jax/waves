## How to Play

### Objective
Break the blocks to reveal the hidden song

### Controls
Choose between
- **Keys**: Use arrow keys or A/D to move the paddle left and right
- **Voice**: Control the paddle with your voice
  - Loud sounds move the paddle right
  - Quiet sounds move the paddle left
  - Silence makes the paddle stop

Release the ball by pressing the space bar

### Gameplay
- Select your controls 
- Complete the calibration wizard to tune voice control
- Use the paddle to bounce the ball towards the blocks
- Don't let the ball fall off the screen
- You have three lives
- Clear all the blocks to win
